Jillian Mhaie, also known as Fleurhaie, is a 19-year-old aspiring artist who finds joy in capturing imagination, emotion, and beauty on canvas.
Inspired by the soft serenity of nature, the whimsical worlds of anime, and the charm of playful characters, she creates paintings that tell stories
and evoke feelings.

Her store, Fleurhaie’s Canvas, is a place where her art comes alive — from dreamy lily ponds and impressionist-inspired landscapes to playful and lively
character portraits. Each painting is shared for fun and connection, inviting viewers to pause, dream, and feel inspired. Through her work,
Fleurhaie hopes to bring moments of wonder, calm, and joy into everyday life, making art a gentle companion for everyone who experiences it.
